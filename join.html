Join the Archduchy of Alhambra

Whether you come seeking history, fellowship, craftsmanship, or adventure, you are welcome here.

The Archduchy of Alhambra is a local chapter of The Adrian Empire, Inc., an international historical society devoted to recreating the culture, arts, and pageantry of medieval and Renaissance Europe.

Our members gather to learn, teach, compete, create, and celebrate together through tournaments, feasts, arts and sciences, music, dance, service, and ceremony. Above all, we are a community built upon courtesy, honor, and friendship.

Your First Visit

You do not need special clothing, armor, equipment, or experience.

Simply come as you are.

You'll meet friendly people who are happy to answer questions and help you discover the parts of the Society that interest you most.
